About us:

Our small shop was born and raised in NYC. We draw inspiration from not only
the video games, manga, anime and pop culture exhibited from our products and their
descriptions, but from the culture of this great city as well.

Our goal is to share our love and interest for these amazing art forms in new
and creative ways never seen before. What better way to do that than
through the one art form almost everyone can agree on:

FOOD! :D
